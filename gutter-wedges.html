Custom Gutter Wedges

Designed and manufactured exclusively for Ascension Gutters & More, LLC to deliver reliable fit and long-term durability across multiple install scenarios.

Contact Me About Wedges
About this project: These wedges were developed in close collaboration with Ascension Gutters & More to solve specific installation challenges while keeping the workflow simple and consistent.

Available Sizes & Options

3" Wedge

Compact lift for tighter runs and soffit constraints. A solid choice when clearance is limited.

3.5" Wedge

Balanced height for common installations. Keeps a clean line while improving water flow angle.

4" Wedge

Standard option when extra clearance is needed. Helps maintain pitch on longer runs.

4" Wedge — 22.5°

Angled variant for transitions and specialty tie-ins where a fixed degree improves alignment.

4" Bracket (5.5" Radius)

Curved support for radius sections. Adds stability without compromising the contour.

4.5" Wedge

Extra lift for challenging pitches or uneven fascia. Useful where added angle is essential.
