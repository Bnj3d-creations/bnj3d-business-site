Returns

We accept returns for defective or incorrect items within 14 days of delivery. Items must be returned in their original condition.

  • Return requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrives defective, damaged, or incorrect, in which case BnJ 3D Creations will provide a prepaid return label.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method after the returned item is received and inspected. Please allow up to 7 business days for processing.
  • Returns on non-defective, non-custom products may be accepted on a case-by-case basis. Standard return shipping costs will apply.

Exchanges

If you received the wrong item or a defective product, we’ll happily exchange it.

  • Exchanges must be requested within 14 days of delivery.
  • Exchanged items must be returned in unused condition.
  • Custom or made-to-order items are not eligible for exchange unless defective.
