Returns

We accept returns for both defective and non-defective products within 14 days of delivery. Items must be returned in their original condition.

  • Return requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Buyer is responsible for return shipping unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • Refunds will be issued after the returned item is received and inspected.

Exchanges

If you ordered the wrong item or received a defective item, we’re happy to exchange it.

  • Exchanges must be requested within 14 days of delivery.
  • Exchanged items must be returned in unused condition.
  • Custom or made-to-order products may not be eligible for exchange unless defective.
