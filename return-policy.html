Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your order. If there’s a problem, we’ll work quickly to resolve it. Refunds are issued in accordance with our Returns policy.

When We Issue Refunds

  • Defective, damaged, or incorrect items: eligible for a refund or replacement if reported within 14 days of delivery.
  • Non-defective items: refunds may be approved case-by-case when items are unused, in original packaging, and returned within the stated window.

Timeline

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll email you about approval.
  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days.
  • Your bank or card issuer may need additional time to post the credit.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or made-to-order items (unless defective).
  • Gift cards.
  • Items not in original condition or returned beyond the stated window.

How to Start

Email us at bnj3dcreations@gmail.com with your order info and photos (if damaged). We’ll provide a return authorization and, when applicable, a prepaid label.

Please also review our Returns and Shipping pages for full details.
