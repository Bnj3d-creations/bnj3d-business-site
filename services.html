What We Offer

“We don’t just print — we bring your ideas to life, one layer at a time.”

3D Model Design

We turn your ideas into precise, print-ready CAD models using tools like Fusion 360. From napkin sketch to digital file — we’ve got you covered.

File Repair & Model Cleanup

Have a broken STL file? We fix holes, wall thickness issues, and mesh errors so your file slices cleanly and prints without issues.

3D Printing Services

We print functional parts, gifts, prototypes, and custom solutions in a variety of materials and colors — including multi-color printing via AMS systems.

Material Options

Choose from PLA, PETG, PETG-CF, ASA, ABS, TPU (95A & 98A), Nylon, and more — suitable for indoor and outdoor use, flexible or rigid parts.

Special Projects

From branded fender badges and custom trailer hitch covers to coasters, holders, and outdoor bird feeders — we create durable, made-to-order solutions for practical, decorative, or display needs. Got something unique in mind? Let’s build something made to last.

Rapid Turnaround & Local Pickup

Located in south Louisiana, we offer fast turnarounds and optional local pickup for quicker service without the shipping wait.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials can you print in?

We print in PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, Nylon, TPU, and specialty carbon-fiber blends — perfect for both decorative and functional parts.

Can you do multi-color prints?

Yes! With our AMS-equipped Bambu A1 printers and Qidi Plus 4, we can produce multi-color designs or mix materials for unique results.

Do you accept custom requests?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom projects — from prototypes to decorative items — turning your idea into a reality.

How long does a typical print take?

It depends on size and complexity. Small prints may take a few hours, while larger or detailed designs may run overnight or longer.
