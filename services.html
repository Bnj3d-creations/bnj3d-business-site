Material Options

  • PLA — Affordable and easy to print. Great for prototypes and indoor use.
  • PETG — Strong, slightly flexible, UV/weather resistant. Ideal for outdoor projects.
  • PETG-CF — PETG blended with carbon fiber for increased strength and stiffness. Excellent for functional parts.
  • ABS — Durable and impact-resistant. Suitable for engineered parts; needs enclosure to avoid warping.
  • ASA — UV and weather-resistant alternative to ABS. Great for outdoor parts.
  • PET — Similar to PETG with different print characteristics; good chemical resistance.
  • Nylon — Strong, flexible, and wear-resistant. Best for mechanical components and hinges.
  • TPU (95A & 98A) — Flexible, rubber-like. Great for gaskets, keychains, and shock-absorbing parts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials can you print in?

We print in PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, Nylon, TPU, and specialty carbon-fiber blends — perfect for both decorative and functional parts.

Can you do multi-color prints?

Yes! With our AMS-equipped Bambu A1 printers and Qidi Plus 4, we can produce multi-color designs or mix materials for unique results.

Do you accept custom requests?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom projects — from prototypes to decorative items — turning your idea into a reality.

How long does a typical print take?

It depends on size and complexity. Small prints may take a few hours, while larger or detailed designs may run overnight or longer.
