📦 Material Options

  • PLA – Affordable and easy to print. Great for prototypes and indoor use.
  • PETG – Strong, slightly flexible, UV/weather resistant. Ideal for outdoor projects.
  • PETG Carbon Fiber – PETG blended with carbon fiber for increased strength and stiffness. Excellent for functional parts.
  • ABS – Durable and impact-resistant. Suitable for engineered parts, but may warp without proper enclosure.
  • ASA – UV and weather resistant alternative to ABS. Great for outdoor-use parts.
  • PET – Similar to PETG but with different printing characteristics. Good chemical resistance.
  • Nylon – Strong, flexible, and wear-resistant. Best for mechanical components and hinges.
  • TPU (95A & 98A) – Flexible, rubber-like material. Great for gaskets, keychains, and shock-absorbing parts.
